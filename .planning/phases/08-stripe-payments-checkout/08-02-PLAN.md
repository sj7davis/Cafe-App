<objective>
Extend the Stripe Checkout flow so gift cards and subscription passes are sold through real Stripe payments (cards/passes created only after the webhook), and so loyalty points redeemed at checkout are decremented from the balance and recorded. Today `venue.createGiftCard` and `venue.purchasePass` create rows directly with NO payment, and `loyalty.getRedemptionValue` only computes a discount preview without ever decrementing points against a paid order.

Purpose: Close PAY-03 (gift card via Stripe), PAY-04 (pass via Stripe), CHK-02 (redeem loyalty points — balance decremented + total reduced).
Output: Two new public checkout-session mutations and webhook branches that fulfil gift card / pass / loyalty on payment success.
</objective>
